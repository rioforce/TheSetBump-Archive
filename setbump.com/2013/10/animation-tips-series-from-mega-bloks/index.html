Animation Tips Series from Mega Bloks

October 8, 2013 | Written by thefourmonkeys | Filed under: General

IÂ  discovered a well done and funny “Animation Tips” video series the other day while looking around sites for brickfilming news. The “Animation Tips” series was made for the HALO Toymation Mega Bloks website and is very educational for both novices and experts of brickfilming. Tyler Gregory, the creator of the animation tips video series, delivers five great short tutorials about stop-motion animation using Mega Bloks bricks as his brick of choice. Tyler’s use of humor and illustrations keep the viewer entertained and informed about the subject.

Tyler Gregory produced the winning entry of the Halo Toymation 2011 contest with his action-thriller The Rookie.
